## Energy Curve
The simulation MUST follow a 4-phase energy arc:
1. **Calm intro (0-15s)**: The intact structure is displayed, perhaps with slight wobble/settling
2. **Building (15-40s)**: Small impacts or cracks begin, tension builds
3. **Climax (40-55s)**: Major destruction event — collapse, explosion, wrecking ball impact
4. **Satisfying resolution (55-{{ target_duration_sec }}s)**: Debris settles, dust clears, rubble rests

## Destruction Design Guidelines
- Build the structure using many small pymunk.Poly bodies (bricks/blocks)
- Bricks should be connected loosely (they separate under force)
- Use pymunk.PinJoint or pymunk.SlideJoint with break_force for breakable joints
- Destruction trigger: heavyweight body (wrecking ball), impulse, or cascading removal
- Debris should scatter realistically with gravity
- Consider particle effects for "dust" (tiny bodies with high damping)
- Colour the structure from the palette, debris can be darker
- The structure should be visually interesting (not just a simple wall)
